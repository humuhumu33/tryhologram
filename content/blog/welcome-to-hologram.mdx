We're excited to launch the Hologram blog! This is where we'll share updates, insights, and articles about our work on building **open source, community-led, decentralized infrastructure** for the future.

## What to Expect

On this blog, you'll find:

- **Technical deep dives** into our architecture and design decisions
- **Community updates** about events, releases, and milestones
- **Research insights** from our team and contributors
- **Best practices** for working with decentralized systems

## Our Mission

At UOR Foundation, we believe in creating a shared language for universal data. Our work focuses on:

1. Building open source tools and protocols
2. Fostering a vibrant community of contributors
3. Advancing research in decentralized systems
4. Making decentralized infrastructure accessible to everyone

## Get Involved

We're always looking for contributors and community members to join us:

- Join our [Discord](https://discord.gg/uor) to connect with the team
- Check out our [GitHub](https://github.com/uor-framework) to contribute code
- Follow along here for regular updates

Thanks for being part of our journey. Stay tuned for more updates!
